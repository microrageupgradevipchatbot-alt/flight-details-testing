SECTION: COMPLAINT_HANDLING
KEYWORDS: complaint, complaint submission, refund claims, complaint review, agent late, agent no show, service issues, booking details, proof required, operational delays, support contact, service team, alternative arrangements, complaint process, customer support

QUESTION: How can a customer submit a complaint?
ANSWER: Customers can submit complaints by contacting support and providing booking details, passenger name, service date, and a description of the issue.

QUESTION: How long does complaint review take?
ANSWER: Complaints should normally be submitted within five days after the service date and will be reviewed according to company policy.

QUESTION: What proof is required for refund claims?
ANSWER: Customers may be asked to provide booking confirmation details, service date information, and supporting details describing the issue.

QUESTION: What happens if the agent arrives late?
ANSWER: If operational delays occur the service team will coordinate with the passenger and adjust the service where possible.

QUESTION: What happens if the agent does not show up?
ANSWER: Passengers should immediately contact the support team using the contact information provided in their booking confirmation.

QUESTION: What happens if airport authorities deny service access?
ANSWER: Services must comply with airport regulations. If authorities deny access due to security or operational reasons the service team will provide alternative arrangements where possible.
