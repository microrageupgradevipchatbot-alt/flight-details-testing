QUESTION: Can VIP services bypass immigration?
ANSWER: No. Immigration procedures are controlled by government authorities and cannot be bypassed by VIP services.

QUESTION: Can VIP services bypass airport security checks?
ANSWER: No. All passengers must pass through airport security checks regardless of VIP services.

QUESTION: Who controls fast-track lanes?
ANSWER: Fast-track lanes are controlled by airport authorities and immigration departments.

QUESTION: What happens if fast-track is unavailable?
ANSWER: If fast-track access is unavailable due to airport authority policies or operational conditions, passengers will still receive full escort assistance through standard airport procedures.

QUESTION: Who provides wheelchair services at the airport?
ANSWER: Wheelchair services are typically provided by the airline and should be requested directly with the airline before travel.

QUESTION: Can VIP agents push wheelchairs?
ANSWER: Depending on airport regulations agents may assist with wheelchairs, but primary wheelchair service is usually provided by airline staff.

QUESTION: Can VIP agents access restricted airside areas?
ANSWER: Access to restricted airside areas depends on security clearance granted by airport authorities.

QUESTION: Is lounge access guaranteed?
ANSWER: Lounge access depends on lounge capacity, airport policies, and the selected service package.

QUESTION: Can immigration refuse entry even with VIP service?
ANSWER: Yes. Immigration authorities retain full control over entry decisions regardless of VIP services.
